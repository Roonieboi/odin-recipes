Blueberry Muffins

Blueberry muffins are delicious and moist baked treats filled with juicy blueberries. They are perfect for breakfast or as a snack.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar (for topping)
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour (for topping)
  • 1/4 cup cold butter (for topping)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon (for topping)

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Grease or line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix vegetable oil, egg, and milk.
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  5. Gently fold in the blueberries.
  6. Fill each muffin cup about two-thirds full with the batter.
  7. In a small bowl, mix together the sugar, flour, butter, and cinnamon for the topping until crumbly.
  8. Sprinkle the topping over each muffin.
  9. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  10. Let the muffins cool in the pan for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
  11. Enjoy your delicious homemade blueberry muffins!
