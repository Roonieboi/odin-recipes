Lasagna

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish made of stacked layers of thin pasta sheets, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce, baked to perfection. It typically includes ingredients such as ground beef or sausage, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, tomato sauce, and various herbs and spices.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 oz each) tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 Egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, salt, sugar, oregano, and pepper. Simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese and egg.
  5. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange a layer of noodles over the sauce. Spread a third of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, then sprinkle with a third of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers two more times.
  6. Cover with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until the lasagna is bubbly and golden.
  7. Let it stand for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
  8. Enjoy!
